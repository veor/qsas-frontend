Quezon Scholarship Application System

The 1987 Constitution of the Republic of the Philippines upholds education as a fundamental right and mandates the State to prioritize, protect, and promote quality education for all citizens. It further requires the government to undertake necessary measures to ensure that education is accessible to all at every level.

In line with this constitutional mandate, the Provincial Government of Quezon (PGQ) has instituted various scholarship programs and educational financial assistance initiatives aimed at providing qualified individuals with access to quality and affordable education. These programs seek to support deserving students, promote equal educational opportunities, and constribute to the development of a skilled and empowered citizenry.
